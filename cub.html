General Infomation

Hermitcraft is a whitelist only minecraft server which started in April 2012 by Generikb which is currently administered by Xisumavoid. They have had several modded and vanilla series, currently in the 9th vanilla season and 11th year of running. It's members, called Hermits are popular minecraft creators who have been recognized by other Hermits and invited to join Hermitcraft.

How they joined.

The Hermits are what the fandom refers to the players on the server. There is currently 25 whitelisted Hermits (23 active, 2 inactive) who often post youtube videos on the server or stream the server on twitch.

Seasons they were apart of.

The hermits are argueable the most important part of the server. With many of them playing for multiple seasons or just a few its important to recognize them all.
